Clarion Alley

Pedestrian street between Mission and Valencia streets and 17th and 18th streets with murals painted by the Clarion Alley Mural Project. CAMP is an artist collective of six artists that began in 1992 with the mission to produce socially engaged and diverse public art. New murals are added to the alley all year long.


Women's Building

This mural is on the outside of a women’s let non-profit and eduction community center. It has been the dedicated to advocating for self-determination, gender equality and social justice since 1979 when the building was bought by the San Francisco Women’s Center. The mural was painted by 7 women in 1994, it is both on the exterior of the building and inside the hallways. The mural highlights icons from history and fixtures with the names over 600 women written in calligraphy.
