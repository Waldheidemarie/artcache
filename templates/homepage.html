Artcache Info


Artcache is an app to discover, log, and share public art! The idea came from geocaching - a digital scavenger hunt. The two goals of this project are to create a database of public art and to allow users to explore the city through finding art.

guide to start artcaching:

map
zoom in on the map to see art sites, click a site for more information and to log a site


see a tour of art through different San Francisco neighborhoods


add a new artwork to the map for others to find

public data sources:

1% Art Program: A city-wide program that requires all projects involving new building in Downtown San Francisco to provide public art equal to 1% of total construction cost. It was established by the 1985 Downtown plan and governed by Section 429 of the Planning Code.

Civic Art: Includes publicly-sited works in the Civic Art Collection including historic monuments, murals, and artworks commissioned through the city's Public Art Program.
