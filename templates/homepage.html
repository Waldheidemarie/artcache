Public Data Sources

1% Art Program: program that requires all projects involving new building in Downtown San Francisco provide public art equal to or at least 1% of total construction cost. It was established by the 1985 Downtown plan and governed by Section 429 of the Planning Code

Civic Art: includes publicly-sites works in the Civic Art Collection including historic monuments, murals, and artworks commissioned through the city's Public Art Program

About Me

My name is Liz, and I made this project as part of the Hackbright Academy Fellowship in June 2019. The idea for my project came from geocaching - a digital scavenger hunt. The two goals of this project are to create a database of public art and to allow users to explore the city through finding art.
